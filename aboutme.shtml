About me. My name is Daniel Gude and I am a first year student studying Software Engineering at RIT. During my 4 years of highschool I rigged, designed, and ran the lighting for my school. I worked on the annual chorus concerts, fall plays, and spring musicals as well as any other event occurring in the auditorium. I have had experience working on ETC's Express 48/96 board and the ETC ION. (Side note the ION runs the same software as the EOS Ti which is the most prevalent board used on Broadway) So far at RIT, I designed a theatre club's musical this past fall. Over the summer of 2016, I was employed by a Lighting and Sound company. In the weeks leading up to the highschool performances, I would be the first to arrive and leave hours after anyone else. Lighting is a passion of mine and I hope you can learn something from this page. My personal website has more information about me and more projects I have done.
